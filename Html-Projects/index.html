Taco Trivia

When did tacos first appear in the United stated?

Tacos first appeared in the United States around 1905, likely due to Mexican migrants bringing them with them as they sought work in the Southwest. These migrants, working in mines, railroads, and other industries, introduced their food traditions, including tacos, to the US. Initially, tacos were seen as a lower-class street food.(Source: The history of the taco )
